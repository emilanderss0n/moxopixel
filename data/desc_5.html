Tactical Gear Component (TGC) is a big mod for new gear, attachments, and more. Almost 100 new items were added through many hours inside Blender, Substance Painter, and Unity. Three new pouch containers are also included in this mod. This is a preview of the items that will be added for an even bigger mod that will be released in the future, Welcome to Tarkov (WTT).

Key features include

  • Adds unique clothing to the game.
  • Adds about 100 new unique weapon attachments and gear to the game.
  • Balanced barters for the trader.
  • Realism patch included.
