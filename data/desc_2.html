Custom trader mod for SPT (Single Player Tarkov). Ivan Samoylov is a master craftsman renowned for his exceptional skill in creating exquisite weapon cosmetics. With an innate talent for blending artistry and functionality, he transforms ordinary weapons into mesmerizing works of art.

Key features include

  • Sells mystery boxes (Warbox and Painter's Special Delivery)
  • Sells items from all my other item mods
  • Multiple custom quests
